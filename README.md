MY FAMILY BUSINESS WEBSITE PROJECT
A modern, responsive, and user-friendly web application designed for a family-owned gardening business or a community garden project. This site serves as a digital storefront and information hub for plant enthusiasts.
 Features
Responsive Design: Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
Service Showcase: Detailed sections for gardening services (landscaping, maintenance, planting).
Product/Plant Gallery: A visually appealing catalog of available plants or completed projects.
Contact Form: Easy-to-use form for inquiries and appointments.
